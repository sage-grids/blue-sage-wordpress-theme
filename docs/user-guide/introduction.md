# Welcome to Blue Sage

Congratulations on choosing **Blue Sage**, a premium WordPress theme designed for AI-powered businesses, SaaS companies, and high-end service brands. 

This guide is designed for non-technical users to help you get the most out of your new website.

## 1. Getting Started
Once your developer has installed the theme, you can activate it by following these steps:
1. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard.
2. Go to **Appearance > Themes**.
3. Find **Blue Sage** and click the **Activate** button.

## 2. The Blue Sage Info Page
We have included a custom info page to help you connect with the theme creators and find support.
- Go to **Appearance > Blue Sage Theme**.
- Here you can find our contact information, social media links, and a brief overview of the theme's philosophy.

## 3. How to Build Your Site
Blue Sage is a **Block Theme**. This means you build your pages using "blocks" (sections of content) like LEGO bricks. 

There are two main ways to create your pages:
1. **Block Patterns:** Pre-designed sections (like a Hero section or a Pricing table) that you can insert with one click. This is the fastest way to build your site.
2. **Custom Blocks:** Individual elements (like an Accordion or a Carousel) that you can add and customize to fit your needs.
