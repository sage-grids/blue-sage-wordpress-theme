# Managing Your Header & Footer

The Header (at the top) and Footer (at the bottom) are the most important navigation sections of your site. In Blue Sage, these are managed within the **Site Editor**.

## 1. Finding Your Header & Footer
1. Go to **Appearance > Editor**.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Patterns**.
3. Under the **"Template Parts"** category, you will find your **Header** and **Footer**.

## 2. Managing Your Navigation Menu
Your main navigation links are in the Header.
1. Click on the **Navigation** block in the Header.
2. To **Add a Link**, click the **(+) Plus Button** that appears at the end of the menu.
3. To **Remove a Link**, click on the link and press the three dots (options) and select **"Remove [Link Name]"**.
4. To **Reorder Links**, click on a link and use the arrows in the toolbar to move it left or right.

## 3. Updating Your Site Title & Logo
- **Site Logo:** Click the logo image in the Header and select **"Replace"** to upload your own.
- **Site Title:** Click on the text next to the logo to change your site's name.

## 4. Customizing Your Footer
Blue Sage includes both a standard Footer and a Minimal Footer.
1. **Navigation Links:** Similar to the Header, you can click on the navigation blocks in the Footer to add or edit links (e.g., "Company," "Product," "Legal").
2. **Social Media Icons:** The Footer includes links to your social media profiles. Click on an icon, then click the **Link Icon** in the toolbar to enter your own social media URL.
3. **Copyright Notice:** Click on the text at the bottom to update the copyright information. Note that the "Designed by SAGE GRIDS" link is included to support the theme creators.
4. **Newsletter Signup:** Some footers include a newsletter form. While this theme provides the design for the form, you may need a developer or plugin to connect it to your specific email service (like Mailchimp).

## 5. Saving Your Changes
When you're finished, click the **Save** button in the top-right corner. Your changes will be applied to every page on your site.
