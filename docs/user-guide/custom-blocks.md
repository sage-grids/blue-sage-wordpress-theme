# Using Custom Blocks

Blue Sage includes custom blocks (starting with **"BS —"**) that have special features and interactivity.

## 1. Finding Custom Blocks
1. Open the page or post you want to edit.
2. Click the **(+) Plus Button** in the top-left corner.
3. Search for "BS" to see all the custom blocks included in this theme.

## 2. Highlighted Blocks

### BS — Blog Cards
This block allows you to show your latest blog posts in different layouts.
- **How to Use:** Add the block, then select the **Layout** in the settings on the right (Standard, Featured, or List).
- **Options:** You can choose a specific category of posts or change how many posts are shown.

### BS — FAQ Accordion
A section for your Frequently Asked Questions that visitors can click to expand.
- **How to Use:** Add the block and start typing your questions and answers.
- **Options:** You can allow multiple items to be open at once or keep it set so that only one item can be expanded at a time.

### BS — Testimonial Carousel
A beautiful, sliding carousel of quotes from your happy customers.
- **How to Use:** Add the block and click on each slide to enter the testimonial text, author name, and company.
- **Options:** You can enable or disable **Autoplay** (to make it slide automatically) and set the delay between slides.

### BS — Pricing Table
Professional tables to show your product's plans and pricing.
- **How to Use:** Add the block and enter your plan details.
- **Options:** You can toggle the **Monthly/Yearly Billing Switch** and highlight a specific plan as "Most Popular."

### BS — Metrics Bar
Animated counters for your site's most important stats (e.g., "1,000+ Customers").
- **How to Use:** Add the block and enter your numbers and labels.
- **Options:** Choose between a dark or light background to match your site's sections.

## 3. Customizing Block Settings
Whenever you select a custom block, you'll see a panel of settings in the **Right Sidebar**. This is where you can find all the options mentioned above.
- If the sidebar is not visible, click the **Settings (Gear) Icon** in the top-right corner.
- Make sure the **Block** tab is selected at the top of the sidebar.
