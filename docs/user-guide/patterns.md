# Building with Block Patterns

The fastest way to build your website is to use **Block Patterns**. These are pre-designed sections (like a Hero section, Pricing table, or Testimonial carousel) that you can insert with a single click.

## 1. How to Insert a Pattern
1. Open the page or post you want to edit.
2. Click the **(+) Plus Button** (Block Inserter) in the top-left corner.
3. Switch to the **Patterns** tab.
4. Click **Explore all patterns**.
5. In the left sidebar, find the categories that start with **"Blue Sage"**.

## 2. Our Pattern Categories
Blue Sage includes many custom-built patterns. Here's a brief overview of what you can find:

- **Blue Sage: Heroes:** Big, beautiful banners to put at the very top of your pages. Includes centered and split-screen layouts.
- **Blue Sage: Features:** Sections to describe your services or features. Includes icon grids and large feature cards.
- **Blue Sage: Social Proof:** Marquees with client logos, animated metrics, and testimonial carousels to build trust with your visitors.
- **Blue Sage: Pricing:** Two and three-tier pricing tables. Many include a "Most Popular" highlight.
- **Blue Sage: Team:** Layouts to showcase your team members with photos and bios.
- **Blue Sage: CTA (Call to Action):** Important sections designed to encourage your visitors to take action (like signing up or contacting you).
- **Blue Sage: Blog:** Pre-built layouts for your blog page, showing your latest posts.

## 3. Customizing Your Patterns
Once you insert a pattern, you can click on any part of it (text, images, or buttons) to change the content to your own.
- To change text, just click and start typing.
- To change an image, click the image and then the **Replace** button.
- To change a button link, click the button and then the **Link** icon in the toolbar.

## 4. Tips for Success
- **Start at the Top:** Insert a "Hero" pattern first to set the tone for your page.
- **Use Spacing:** If patterns feel too close together, use the **Spacer** block to add some room between them.
- **Stay on Brand:** All patterns use the theme's colors and fonts automatically, so your site will always look consistent.
